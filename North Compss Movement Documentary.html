"  Lulusong,  Aahon Bayan ng Hagonoy " 

       One of the districts in Bulacan where high tide is experienced almost all the time is Hagonoy. Imagine how stressful it is for the people living there especially the ones whose houses are located in the lower grounds at the location.

        We interviewed three people from barangay San Jose: a variety store owner, a 6th grade student and the barangay captain of San Jose. They stated their own different experiences whenever a high tide or flood is witnessed. Marites La Paz, who is the vendor and owner of the store, told how she manages the condition by adjusting to the circumstance. She said that it is hard for her because there are times that she lifts the table (where her products are put on) on her own. Doing her business is a hazard because she has to undergo walking in dirty waters to make money. Her business is also unsecure because no much people might want to trust buying her products because of the location of her business. Aaron Mordido, the student, stated that usually, when the water is 

high, their school opt to cancel classes and it is a big opportunity loss for students because instead of gaining new lessons, they just had to stay at home and be safe which is okay because they'd be much safer. The barangay captain is named Jorge Eligio. He told us his platforms which is about how to make their barangay a less polluted place. THe barangay captain wanted the pile of rubbish to be taken care of with the help of garbage trucks and he made it possible for the barangay's main road to be cemented so that there will be at least no flood on main pathways.
      We became aware and informed about the situations the people in Hagonoy are undergoing. We hope that more people become responsible with their trash because even though it is just one trash, it could still add up and turn to a lot of garbage if other people become irresponsible of their trash too. We can start the change by deciding in ourselves to change. One small move of change is a step to a huge change.
