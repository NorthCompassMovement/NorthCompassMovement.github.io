       We chose this movement because we want to help the environment to return how wonderful it was from the beginning. What we want is to see our mother nature heal unceasingly as time passes by, not otherwise. Our natural environment is an abundant blessing so we must treasure it, beautify and not destroy it again.

      Wastes are very conspicuous nowadays. People’s carelessness is the cause of the devastation of nature. We tend to think that by simply not throwing trash in its proper place is okay when it’s done minimally but truth is, it’s terrible. Our simple actions, when put together to help contribute good things in nature, will be a great help.
      It is for sure that all of us experienced floods when there is a heavy rain. Floods may seem normal to some areas but it is also a sign that we should fix our ways to make the world a better place, literally. Many people opt to neglect these signs and some might not have the idea that flood is one of the effects of rubbish scattered everywhere. 

      These trash get stuck in drainages, flowing in rivers and bays and can be seen almost everywhere. Due to this, we see that flood water is very dirty and it cannot flow freely in drainage systems. Also, there are no enough trees to absorb flood waters. Pollutions circulate in a cycle after all.  


      Some of us also experiences/experienced high tides. It is a difficult situation because even if it did not rain, there is still plenty of water outside our houses and there are times that water goes inside houses. Occurrence change. Before, even if there is a high tide, the water is not that high, it is shallow, but that is not the case nowadays. People should stop neglecting these consequences.
Who would not want a better future right? We want better days ahead of us. We hope to make a change through campaign and we want to pursue change. Change starts once we decide in ourselves.
